Online reservation

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

can i make an online appointment by email or phone?

Currently, we do not accept online appointments by email or phone. For new reservation, please select your preferred clinic and make a reservation.

If you can't make a reservation from the page above, try changing the device (PC/smartphone) you are accessing or changing your browser (Safari or Google Chrome).

If you still can't, please contact online support with the following:

1. your name
2. your device (iPhone11, Sony smartphone, etc. should be described in detail)
3. your browser

where can i change or cancel my reservation?

please log in

we do not accept reservation changes or cancellations by e-mail or telephone

is it possible to make an online reservation even if it is my first visit?

patients who visit for the first time can also make appointments for medical care via the internet.

what is the benefit to signing up?

view the medical information

is my information secure?

Yes. your information safe from unauthorized users.

how do i sign up?

Click Login on the right-hand menu, and click Membership Registration. Follow the instructions to sign up.

how can i get help if i have a question?

click the Contact Us page at the top of the screen . After you submit the form, you will receive a response via email within two business days.
